About this Project

The task of this project is to develop a website for a fictitious company, Innovate Training, that provides training courses for businesses and students. The website will be used by the company to promote its services and to allow customers to book courses online. The website will also be used by the company to manage its courses and customers.

My Role and Solutions

As this is a individual work, I am the only developer for this project. I am responsible for the design, development and testing of the website. I have used HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP and MySQL to develop the website.

Creating visuals

After the data was cleaned and transformed, I created the visuals for the dashboard. As I was in charge of analysing the customers, these are the areas I needed to focus on as stated by the requirements:

  • A visualisation showing the sales in bins of $500 for all the orders. Sales amounts above $4500 will be grouped under one bin
  • A visualisation showing the number of orders placed by each customer
  • A visualisation showing the sales amount gained from each customer.
  • Include necessary filters to enable users to filter the data.
